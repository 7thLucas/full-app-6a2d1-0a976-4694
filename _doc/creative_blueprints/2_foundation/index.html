Why this app exists

✕ The Problem
  • ▸ Shoppers open 5–8 browser tabs to compare prices across Amazon, Flipkart, and others — every single time.
  • ▸ Shipping costs are hidden until checkout — what looks cheaper often isn't once delivery is added.
  • ▸ Coupon codes are scattered, expired, or missed — most shoppers never apply the best available discount.
  • ▸ The result: decision fatigue, overspending, and wasted time on a task that should take seconds.
✓ The Solution
  • ▸ One search surfaces every major retailer simultaneously — no tab-switching, no manual hunting.
  • ▸ True landed price shown upfront per store: base price + delivery charge, visible before you tap Buy Now.
  • ▸ Active coupons surfaced automatically with discount pre-calculated — the final saving is clear at a glance.
  • ▸ One-tap "Buy Now" deep link sends users directly to the retailer product page, ready to checkout.
From search to best deal in under 10 seconds
