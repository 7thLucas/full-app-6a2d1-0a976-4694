Post-MVP Roadmap

What comes next for ThriftFam

3 High-Value Features
01 Price Drop Alerts

Track any product. Get notified the moment the price drops.

Set a personal target price threshold. The moment any tracked store's true landed price meets it, a push notification fires — with the winning store, new price, and a one-tap Buy Now deep link.

Multi-product tracking Push notifications Threshold targeting
02 Deal Feed & Flash Sales

A living home feed of trending deals and time-limited flash sales.

Real-time curated feed of hot picks by category, with countdown timers on flash sales. Filter by interest. Save any deal straight to the wishlist from the feed.

Real-time updates Flash countdowns Category filters
03 Smart Wishlist

Save products. See price history. Know the best time to buy.

Each wishlisted item shows a price history chart — highs and lows across all tracked stores over time. Turn guesswork into data-backed purchase decisions.

Price history chart Cross-store lows Personal saves
