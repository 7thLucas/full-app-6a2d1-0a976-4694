​"Create a mobile e-commerce price comparison and deal finder application. The app needs three main screens. First, a Home Screen with a clean, prominent search bar at the top and a grid below showing popular trending product categories. Second, a Results Screen that displays the searched product at the top, followed by a vertical list of different online stores (like Amazon and Flipkart) where it is available. Each store card must display the store logo, the total price including a shipping/delivery charge layer, a list of active coupons with a calculated discount value, and a prominent 'Buy Now' button that acts as a deep link. Third, a clean onboarding screen that requests user permissions for location access and push notifications."
