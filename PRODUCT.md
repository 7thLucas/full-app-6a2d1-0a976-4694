# DealRadar — Product Overview

## What It Is
DealRadar is a mobile e-commerce price comparison and deal finder app. It aggregates product listings across major online stores (Amazon, Flipkart, and others), surfaces the true total cost (base price + shipping + applied coupons), and helps users buy at the lowest price with a single tap.

## Target Audience
- **Gen Z deal hunters**: Value-conscious, mobile-first shoppers who expect speed and visual clarity.
- **Busy professionals**: Need a quick, trustworthy answer — no tab-switching or coupon-hunting.
- **Niche hobbyists** (gamers, sneakerheads): Frequently buy specific SKUs and care deeply about price differences across platforms.

## Core Screens

### 1. Onboarding Screen
- Requests user permission for **location access** (to surface region-specific deals and delivery estimates) and **push notifications** (for price-drop alerts).
- Minimal, trust-building design — explains the "why" behind each permission ask.

### 2. Home Screen
- Prominent **search bar** at the top — the primary entry point.
- **Grid of popular trending product categories** below (e.g., Electronics, Sneakers, Gaming, Fashion, Beauty, Home).
- Clean, energetic layout that appeals to a younger demographic.

### 3. Results Screen (Day-One Priority)
- Searched product name shown clearly at the top.
- **Vertical list of store cards** — one per retailer (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.).
- Each store card shows:
  - Store logo
  - Base price
  - Shipping / delivery charge
  - **True total price** (base + shipping, post-coupon)
  - Active coupons with calculated discount value
  - Prominent **"Buy Now" deep-link button**
- Cards are sorted by true total price (lowest first) by default.

## Brand & Tone
- Name: **DealRadar**
- Tone: Energetic, trustworthy, no-nonsense. Feels like a smart friend who always finds the best deal.
- Anti-references: Avoid cluttered coupon-site aesthetics (coupondunia vibes). No dark-pattern UX.

## Strategic Principles
1. **True price transparency** — always show what the user actually pays.
2. **Speed** — results load fast; skeleton states prevent perceived lag.
3. **Trust** — clear sourcing, no fake discounts, honest coupon states.
4. **One-tap to purchase** — the "Buy Now" button is the hero CTA on every results card.
